recommendation

Option 02 — Fraunces, italic, weight 900.

Fraunces at its heaviest italic is one of the most fashion-current serifs on the internet right now. You see this letterform across indie fashion labels, magazine covers, album art, gallery brands. It's bold without being heavy-handed, serif-warm without being soft, editorial without being precious. The italic gives it movement and attitude.

Kept stacked, the silhouette still reads as your mark — the shape is familiar — but the voice goes from industrial to fashion-editorial. Against a madder-red or indigo-blue background, on a hang tag, a t-shirt, an event invite, it holds up. It has personality. It's confident about its own beauty. That's the brief.
