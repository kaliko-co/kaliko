How I work

Every commission starts with a brief and a design for approval, followed by a dye test on your fabric. I sample, iterate where it helps, and produce in small batches.

Natural fibers onlyOnly natural fibers take plant dye: cotton, linen, silk, wool, hemp, ramie. Polyester, nylon and other synthetics don't. Consider checking your seam threads' fiber content.

Small batchI offer sampling, prototyping, recipe development and limited runs. Kaliko cannot process mass production inquiries.

Color is approximateI advise you on the most suitable dye sources and develop a palette that feels right for your project. Exact Pantone matching is not possible.

Allow timeA commission typically takes several weeks. Natural dyeing is a slow, multi-step process that can be rushed only at the expense of quality.
